About Us

Welcoming Since 2004

Dolce Vita is an Italian cuisine restaurant that serves various Italian dishes. Welcoming everyone of all backgrounds and species! We specialize in serving humans and animals with accommodating dishes for each selection of species. It started when a humans greatest companion wasn't allowed inside stores and restaurants, we understand there is a limit. But must that limit stop at food? Our place is pet friendly because animals are just as special as the people who care for them!
